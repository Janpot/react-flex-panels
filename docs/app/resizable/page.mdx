Interactive panels that can be resized by dragging the resizer handles. Works with both horizontal and vertical layouts.

## How it works

- Place `<Resizer />` components between panels to make them resizable
- Use `minSize` and `maxSize` props to constrain resizing
- The resizer automatically detects the parent's flex direction (horizontal or vertical)
- Panels maintain their constraints while being resized
